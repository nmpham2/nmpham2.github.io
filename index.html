Covidulator - 2nd Place Winner of Jam4Change 2021

Feb. 2021

This project was completed in 5 days and done in collaboration with two of my friends as a submission to ICSSC's Jam4Change, a webjam dedicated to creating projects which invoke social change. Covidulator utilizes React, HTML, and CSS. What the project does: taking inspiration from the Carbon Footprint Calculator, the Covidulator allows for users to input their habits during the Covid-19 pandemic in order to determine how much of an impact they are making on reducing rates, and what they can do to improve based on their results.
